EquiData Form — Delete Account

If you would like to delete your EquiData Form account and associated data, please send a deletion request from your registered email address to:

Email: webapps.rvcits@gmail.com

How to request deletion

Send an email to the address above with the subject line "Account Deletion Request", including the email address registered to your EquiData Form account. We will confirm your identity and process the request.

What gets deleted

Your account profile, login credentials, and personal information (name, contact details, uploaded images) are permanently deleted from our systems.

What may be retained

Records tied to company/payroll/transaction history (e.g. attendance, payroll, or filing records associated with your company account) may be retained as required for legal, audit, or business record-keeping obligations, even after your personal account is deleted.

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 business days of confirmation.
